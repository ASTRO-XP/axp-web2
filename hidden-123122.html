What is Astro XP?


ASTRO XP is an open-world RPG where players can fulfill their fantasies as space settlers and create immersive gaming experiences together through the creation of worlds within worlds.

Choose Your Ride

HOLO-V Classes

XERPENT

When it comes to a HOLO-V with enduring engine power through higher speeds, Xerpent remains incomparable--like riding and leading a dragon--but this time, around the HOLOTRAXX.

SAGITTA

HOLOTRAXX is filled with obstacles that take sharp, neckbreaking turns to surpass. With Sagitta's responsiveness to user input, get an experience of driving a HOLO-V that understands your move.

VALKO

If there is a splitting soul version of a mythical bird Phoenix, it is Valko and it has revived with vengeance in its eyes at HOLOTRAXX. Warning: Not for the faintest of hearts and are strictly for natural drifters of the human-kind ONLY.

SABER

Riding Saber--the tankiest HOLO-V--reduces your opponents' approach in the game into two: whether it is by beating you through the finish line or you beat them before they even get a glimpse of the finish line. The good thing about that is both situations are ideal for you.
